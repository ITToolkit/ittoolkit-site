01 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ITToolkit ("the app", "we") handles information when you use the application on your Android device.

ITToolkit is a professional network diagnostics toolkit providing DNS lookups, port scanning, traceroute, SSL certificate inspection, HTTP analysis, Wi‑Fi diagnostics, DNS propagation checking and CSV data export.

The developer of ITToolkit does not operate servers that collect or store personal user data. The majority of information processed by the app remains on your device at all times.

02 Information the App May Access

Depending on the features you use, ITToolkit may access the following categories of information:

  • Network information — hostnames, domain names, IP addresses, URLs and port numbers entered by you when running diagnostic tools.
  • Wi‑Fi information — network names (SSIDs), access point identifiers (BSSIDs), signal strength, frequency, channel and nearby network scan results when using the Wi‑Fi Scanner.
  • Location context — approximate location data used by Android to support Wi‑Fi scanning and, optionally, to display regional context such as city or country within the Wi‑Fi diagnostic view.
  • App state and settings — user preferences, onboarding completion status, custom DNS resolver settings, free usage counters and Pro upgrade status.
  • User‑entered data — saved hosts, bookmarks, lookup history and SSL certificate watchlist entries stored at your direction.
  • Crash and performance data — anonymised diagnostic information collected automatically by Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics (see Section 6).

ITToolkit does not track your location continuously and does not retain location history. Location access is used only at the point of Wi‑Fi scanning and is not stored beyond the current session.

03 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device to support app functionality:

  • Lookup history and tool results (Pro feature)
  • Saved hosts and bookmarks (Pro feature)
  • SSL certificate watchlist entries (Pro feature)
  • App preferences and display settings
  • Free usage counters and Pro upgrade status
  • CSV files exported to your device's Downloads folder

This data remains on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the application. Exported CSV files are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to the developer.

Encrypted Storage

Pro upgrade status and usage data are protected using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences with AES‑256 encryption, preventing unauthorised access even on rooted devices.

04 Permissions Used

Internet

Required to perform all network diagnostic operations including DNS lookups, SSL certificate checks, HTTP inspection, port scanning and traceroute.

Network State & Wi‑Fi State

Used to detect network connectivity and read Wi‑Fi connection details for diagnostic tools.

Access Fine Location

Android requires this permission to perform Wi‑Fi network scanning. The app may also use it to display contextual location information within the Wi‑Fi Scanner. Location data is not stored persistently or transmitted to the developer.

Nearby Wi‑Fi Devices (Android 13+)

Required on Android 13 and above to read Wi‑Fi network names (SSIDs) during scanning. This permission is declared with the neverForLocation flag, confirming it is not used to determine your location.

Post Notifications

Used solely for optional SSL certificate expiry alerts for domains you have added to the watchlist. You can withdraw this permission at any time in your device settings without affecting any other app functionality.

05 How Information Is Used

Information accessed by ITToolkit is used only for the following purposes:

  • Executing the network diagnostic tools you request
  • Displaying Wi‑Fi and network diagnostic information
  • Persisting your preferences, history and saved data locally
  • Generating CSV exports on your device
  • Delivering optional SSL certificate expiry notifications
  • Processing in‑app purchases and restoring prior purchases via Google Play
  • Presenting optional in‑app review prompts via Google Play
  • Identifying and resolving application crashes via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Understanding aggregate, anonymised usage patterns to guide future development via Firebase Analytics

No information is used for advertising, user profiling or marketing purposes.

06 External Network Requests

As a network diagnostic application, ITToolkit initiates outbound connections as a direct consequence of the tools you use. The following connections may be made:

  • DNS tools query the resolver specified by you (default or custom)
  • HTTP Inspector connects to URLs you provide
  • SSL Checker connects to the domain you specify to retrieve certificate data
  • Port Scanner and Traceroute connect to hosts and IPs you enter
  • DNS Propagation checks query multiple public resolvers worldwide
  • Speed Test connects to Cloudflare measurement endpoints
  • Google Play handles billing, purchase restoration and review prompts

All connections initiated by the app use HTTPS where applicable. Connections to user‑specified hosts follow the protocol and port chosen by you.

Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics

ITToolkit uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, services operated by Google LLC. These services automatically collect the following anonymised data:

  • Crash reports and diagnostic stack traces when the app encounters an error
  • App performance and stability metrics
  • Anonymised usage events indicating which tools are accessed (no user‑entered content is included)

This data is transmitted to Google's servers and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. It is used solely for app stability and improvement and is not used for advertising or sold to any third party.

ITToolkit does not sell personal data. The app does not share user data with third parties for advertising, tracking or profiling purposes.

08 Security

The following measures are in place to protect your data:

  • Sensitive local data (Pro status, usage counts) is stored using AES‑256 EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • All outbound connections made by the app use HTTPS
  • No personal data is transmitted to the developer's servers

Whilst these measures are in place, no system can be guaranteed completely secure. In the event of a security concern, please contact support@ittoolkit.app.

09 Your Rights (UK GDPR)

If you are located in the United Kingdom, you have rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, including the right to:

  • Access personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data

As the majority of data processed by ITToolkit is stored locally on your own device, you can exercise your right to deletion at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the application.

For data held by third‑party services such as Firebase, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and their available data deletion tools.

To exercise any of the above rights or to raise a concern, please contact us at support@ittoolkit.app.

10 Children's Privacy

ITToolkit is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at support@ittoolkit.app and we will take appropriate steps to remove that information.
