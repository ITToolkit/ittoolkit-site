1. Introduction

IT Toolkit ("the App") is a professional network diagnostics application for Android, developed and maintained by IT Toolkit ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights in relation to that information.

By using IT Toolkit, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

Our contact email for all privacy-related enquiries is: support@ittoolkit.app

2. Summary

In plain English — we collect nothing about you, track nothing, and share nothing.

  • We do not collect your personal data.
  • We do not track you.
  • We do not share any data with third parties, except as described in Section 5 (Google Play Billing).
  • All network diagnostics you run stay entirely on your device.
  • We have no servers that receive or store your scan results.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

IT Toolkit does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • The hostnames, IP addresses, or domains you scan
  • Your network configuration or WiFi details
  • Any scan results or diagnostic output

Everything you type into the App and every result it returns exists only on your device and is never transmitted to us.

4. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us:

4.1 Scan History (Pro users only)

If you have purchased IT Toolkit Pro, your scan history is stored in a local database on your device using Android's Room database library. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time from the History screen.

4.2 Pro Purchase Status

A flag indicating whether you have purchased IT Toolkit Pro is stored in your device's shared preferences. This is used solely to unlock Pro features and is never transmitted to us.

4.3 Free Tier Usage Counts

For free users, a count of how many scans you have performed on each tool is stored locally on your device. This is used to enforce the 10 free scan limit per tool. This data is never transmitted to us.

5. Google Play Billing

IT Toolkit offers a one-time in-app purchase ("IT Toolkit Pro") priced at £7.99, processed entirely through Google Play Billing. When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is handled by Google LLC, not by us.
  • We do not receive or store your payment details, billing address, or any financial information.
  • Google Play provides us only with confirmation that a purchase was made and the associated product ID ("pro_unlock"), which we use to unlock Pro features on your device.
  • Google's handling of your payment data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

For questions about a purchase, please contact Google Play support or email us at support@ittoolkit.app.

6. App Permissions

IT Toolkit requests the following Android permissions. Each is used solely for the stated purpose:

INTERNET

Required to perform all network diagnostic functions — DNS lookups, port scanning, traceroute, SSL certificate checks, HTTP inspection, DNS propagation checks, and WiFi speed tests. No personal data is transmitted through this permission.

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Used to check whether an internet connection is available before attempting a scan, so the App can display an appropriate error rather than timing out silently.

ACCESS_WIFI_STATE CHANGE_WIFI_STATE

Used by the WiFi Scanner tool to read details of your connected network and scan nearby access points. This information is displayed to you within the App and is never transmitted to us.

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION

Android requires Location permission to read the names (SSIDs) of nearby WiFi networks — this is a mandatory Android OS requirement. IT Toolkit does not use this permission to determine, record, or transmit your physical location. Your location is never stored or shared.

7. Children's Privacy

IT Toolkit is a professional tool intended for adults — specifically IT professionals, system administrators, and network engineers. It is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us at support@ittoolkit.app.

8. Your Rights

Since IT Toolkit does not collect or process your personal data on our servers, most data subject rights under UK GDPR and equivalent legislation do not apply in the traditional sense. However:

  • You can delete all locally stored scan history from within the App at any time (History → Clear History).
  • You can uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • You can contact us at support@ittoolkit.app with any privacy-related questions or concerns.

If you are located in the UK or EEA and believe we have not complied with applicable law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the UK this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

9. Data Security

Because IT Toolkit does not transmit personal data to any server, there is no server-side data to secure. All data stored locally on your device is protected by Android's built-in application sandboxing — it cannot be accessed by other apps without your device being compromised.

10. Third-Party Services

IT Toolkit interacts with the following third-party services as part of its diagnostic functions:

  • Google DNS-over-HTTPS (doh.dns.google) — used by the DNS Lookup and DNS Propagation tools. Queries are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Public DNS resolvers (Cloudflare 1.1.1.1, Quad9, OpenDNS, and others) — used by the DNS Propagation tool. Each resolver has its own privacy policy.
  • Hosts you scan — the App connects directly to any hostname or URL you enter. We have no control over those third-party servers.

These connections are initiated by you when you run a scan and exist solely to return diagnostic results to you within the App.

Continued use of IT Toolkit after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
