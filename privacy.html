1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ITToolkit handles information when you use the application.

ITToolkit is a network and troubleshooting toolkit providing features such as DNS lookups, port scanning, traceroute, SSL checks, HTTP inspection, Wi‑Fi analysis, DNS propagation checks, and CSV exports.

The developer of ITToolkit does not operate servers that collect or store personal user data from the app. Most information used by the app remains on the device.

2. Information the App May Access

Depending on which features you use, ITToolkit may access:

  • Network information such as hostnames, domains, IP addresses, URLs, and ports entered by the user.
  • Wi‑Fi information including SSID, BSSID, signal strength, frequency, and scan results when the Wi‑Fi scanner is used.
  • Location-related information used by Android to support Wi‑Fi scanning and to optionally display location context such as region or country.
  • App usage and settings including preferences, onboarding completion, custom DNS settings, free usage counters, and Pro purchase status.
  • User-entered content such as saved hosts, bookmarks, lookup history, and SSL watchlist entries.

ITToolkit does not continuously track location and does not store location history. Location access is only used when required for Wi‑Fi related functionality.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Some information is stored locally on your device so the app can function properly.

  • Lookup history and tool results
  • Saved hosts and bookmarks
  • SSL certificate watchlist entries
  • App preferences and settings
  • Free usage counters and Pro unlock status
  • CSV files exported to the device Downloads folder

This information remains on your device unless you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the application.

Exported files are stored only on the user’s device and are not transmitted to the developer.

4. Permissions Used

Internet

Required for network diagnostic tools including DNS lookups, SSL checks, HTTP inspection, port scans, traceroute, billing, and review features.

Network and Wi‑Fi State

Used to detect connectivity status and read Wi‑Fi network information for diagnostic tools.

Location

Android requires location permission to perform Wi‑Fi scanning. ITToolkit may also use this permission to display contextual location information for Wi‑Fi analysis features.

Notifications

Used for optional SSL certificate expiry alerts for domains added to the watchlist.

5. How Information Is Used

  • Running the diagnostic tools requested by the user
  • Displaying Wi‑Fi and network information
  • Saving local preferences and history
  • Creating CSV exports on the device
  • Sending optional SSL expiry notifications
  • Supporting Google Play billing and purchase restoration
  • Allowing optional in-app reviews through Google Play

6. External Network Requests

Because ITToolkit is a network utility app, it sends requests from your device to the systems required to perform the tool you selected.

  • DNS tools query DNS resolvers
  • HTTP Inspector connects to URLs entered by the user
  • SSL checks connect to target hosts
  • Port scans and traceroute connect to target IPs or domains
  • Google Play services handle billing and review features

These requests are necessary for the functionality of the tools.

7. Data Sharing

ITToolkit does not sell personal data.

The app does not intentionally share user data with third parties for advertising or tracking.

Network tools may connect directly to external systems specified by the user as part of normal internet communication.

8. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect locally stored information. However, no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

9. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

If you are located in the United Kingdom, you may have rights under UK data protection law including the right to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data where applicable.

10. Children's Privacy

ITToolkit is not intended for children under the age of 13.
